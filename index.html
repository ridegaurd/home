Frequently Asked Questions

How does RideGuard detect accidents?

RideGuard uses a sophisticated combination of gyroscopic sensors, accelerometers, and AI algorithms to detect abnormal movements and impacts. The system continuously analyzes motion patterns at 500 times per second, distinguishing between normal riding maneuvers and potential accidents with 99.7% accuracy. Our proprietary algorithm can predict falls 0.3 seconds before impact occurs.

How long does the protection system last?

The standard battery provides approximately 30 hours of continuous active monitoring. The system automatically enters low-power mode when not in motion, extending battery life to about 2 weeks between charges. The air cushions maintain protective pressure for up to 24 hours after deployment, and replacement cartridges are easily installed in seconds.

Is RideGuard compatible with all types of two-wheelers?

Yes! RideGuard is designed as a universal safety solution. The mounting system adapts to all motorcycle, scooter, and e-bike frames. The protective gear is fully adjustable for riders from 5'0" to 6'8" in height. We offer custom fitting services for professional racing applications and unique vehicle configurations.

How much maintenance does RideGuard require?

RideGuard requires minimal maintenance. We recommend a monthly system check through our mobile app, which performs a comprehensive diagnostic in under 30 seconds. The air canisters are easily replaceable after deployment and have a 5-year shelf life. The outer shell is weather-resistant and can be cleaned with a damp cloth.

What happens during minor falls that don't require deployment?

RideGuard's AI is specifically trained to distinguish between minor incidents and serious accidents. For minor falls, the system will vibrate to alert you to check for damage but won't deploy the protection. All incidents are logged in the app for review. You can always manually trigger the emergency alert if the situation requires it.
